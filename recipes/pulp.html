Pulp

Pulp began in 1979. They were unsuccessful for many years until they figured out Britpop in 1994 on their album His 'n Hers. They did this by combining extremely catchy pop disco with post-punk qualities like "inappropriate" lyrics and lots of reverb to make the songs darker. They put a finer point on their new sound with their 1995 follow-up Different Class.
